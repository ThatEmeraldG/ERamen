Welcome to ERamen

A Journey into Authentic Ramen Delights!

Discover the Essence of Authentic Ramen

Step into ERamen, where tradition and taste come together to create an extraordinary dining experience.

Established in 1910 in Japan and proudly brought to Indonesia in 2015, ERamen is a legendary ramen shop known for its dedication to quality and craftsmanship.

At ERamen, we craft each bowl of ramen with love and precision. Our noodles, made from organic flour, are transformed into delicious strands that captivate the senses. With an array of mouthwatering flavors and an unwavering commitment to authenticity, we offer an unforgettable taste of Japan's culinary heritage.
